Mesaio Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mesaio ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our application.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Mesaio. For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at: michele.restuccia.ios.developer@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, and account details. We also collect limited technical and usage data to improve the performance and reliability of our services.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract (to provide the Mesaio service)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve and secure our services)
  • Legal obligations
  • Your consent, where applicable

4. How We Use Information

We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve Mesaio, provide customer support, ensure security, and enhance the user experience.

5. Third-Party Services

Mesaio uses trusted third-party services such as Firebase and Crashlytics to monitor app performance, detect crashes, and improve stability. These services may collect anonymized technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when required by law or when necessary to provide the service (e.g., infrastructure providers).

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted.

8. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account at any time directly within the app. When you initiate account deletion, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

10. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability

11. Children's Privacy

Mesaio is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

13. Contact
